Banana Pie

Ingredients

	4 bananas cutted into slices
	1 1/2 cup of wheat flour
	1/2 cup of brown sugar
	2 full soap spoons of butter
	1 tea spoon of cinnamon

Method

	1. Anoit a pan with butter and put the slices of bananas in it
	2. Moist all the dry ingredients with the butter until all form a powder.
	3. Put this powder into the pan up the banana.
	4. Carry the pan in an oven by 30 minutes.
	5. After that, it is ready!
